The Process

How to start a partnership

Formal partnerships are built deliberately — here's the general path from first contact to launch.

Reach out

Share who you are and what kind of collaboration you have in mind through our Partner form.

Explore alignment

We discuss shared goals and how the collaboration fits our strategic pillars and your priorities.

Formalize the partnership

Once details are agreed, we put the collaboration in writing so both sides are clear on scope and expectations.

Launch and report

The partnership goes live, with regular updates so progress and impact stay visible to everyone involved.

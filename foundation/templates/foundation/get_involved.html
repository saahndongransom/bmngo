Common Questions

Before you reach out

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No — we welcome all skill levels. Tell us your interests and availability, and we'll match you to something that fits.

Can I choose which programme I support?

Yes. When you donate or partner with us, you can let us know if you'd like your contribution directed to a specific pillar or initiative.

How quickly will I hear back?

Every submission is saved and reviewed by our team, who will follow up directly using the contact details you provide.

I represent an organization, not an individual — where do I start?

Head to the Partner page and share a bit about your organization and how you'd like to collaborate.

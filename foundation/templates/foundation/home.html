Our Vision

A prosperous, inclusive, and resilient Africa where every young person and vulnerable individual has access to opportunities, skills, and support to realize their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower young people, women, and vulnerable communities through entrepreneurship, leadership, education, humanitarian assistance, and sustainable community initiatives that foster independence and lasting transformation.

About the Foundation

Youth-led. Community-rooted. Built on belief in Africa's greatest asset — its people.

The BM Seed of Hope Foundation (BMSOHF) is a youth-led, non-profit, non-political, and humanitarian organization committed to empowering young people and transforming vulnerable communities through sustainable development initiatives.

We recognize that many young people possess the talent, ambition, and creativity to succeed but are often constrained by unemployment, limited access to quality education, poverty, and social inequality. Our work bridges that gap.

Common Questions

Before you give or get involved.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

This depends on your country of residence and applicable local law. Please contact us for documentation to confirm eligibility in your jurisdiction.

How do I know my donation is used well?

We publish programme updates and impact figures, and donors can request a breakdown of how funds are allocated across our five strategic pillars.

Can my organization partner with BM Seed of Hope?

Yes. We welcome partnerships with institutions on programme delivery, funding, or technical assistance. Reach out through our partnership page to start a conversation.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes, both remote and in-country volunteering opportunities are available. Apply through our volunteer page and our team will follow up with next steps.
